Hobbies

Music

I have been playing music as long as I can remember. Having been raised in a house where both of my parents were amateur musicians I developed a love of music before I knew how to speak. I have studied both formally and informally my entire life and cannot live without it. Some might call me a multi-instrumentalist. I play the bass, guitar and keyboard mostly these days, though there was once a point where I was in college on a trumpet scholarship. Be it performance, music apppreciation, music theory or critique, music is my first love and will always help carry me through.

Food

Is food a hobby? It can be! I do love to eat (no one that sees me will doubt that) but I also love to cook. I am the more domestic partner in my household so you can often find me in the evenings preparing our meal or find me zoned out on a food blog looking for new ways to prepare an ingredient. I am also into brewing beer (and drinking it).

Pets
